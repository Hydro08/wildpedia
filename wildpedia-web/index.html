Endemic to the island of Java, Indonesia, this majestic raptor features dark brown feathers, a prominent head crest, and piercing yellow eyes. It thrives in tropical rainforests and is an apex predator in its habitat.

Cross River Gorilla

The Cross River Gorilla is a rare and shy subspecies found in the mountain forests along the Nigeria Cameroon border. It helps maintain forest balance by spreading seeds through its fruit-based diet.

Mandrill

The Mandrill is the world’s most colorful primate, found in Central Africa’s rainforests. Males have bright blue and red faces used for communication. They live in large troops and help forests thrive by spreading seeds through their diet.

Fennex Fox

The Fennec Fox is the world’s smallest fox, found in the Sahara Desert. Its large ears release heat and help it hear prey underground. With fur that reflects sunlight and protects its feet, this nocturnal fox survives harsh desert life with little water.

Peacock Mantis Shrimp

The Peacock Mantis Shrimp is a colorful marine crustacean found in the Indo-Pacific. Known for its rainbow shell and powerful punch that can crack shells or glass, it also has extraordinary vision, making it one of the ocean’s most fascinating predators.

ABOUT

We created this website to share our fascination with the world’s rare and hard-to-find animals. Wildpedia is a digital encyclopedia that focuses on these extraordinary species across the globe. Our goal is to help people discover, learn, and appreciate animals that are often forgotten or unknown. Through carefully curated information and captivating images, we aim to bring attention to creatures that deserve recognition and protection. Each page of Wildpedia is designed to educate and inspire curiosity about the hidden wonders of our planet’s wildlife. We believe that understanding these rare animals is the first step toward preserving their existence for future generations.

MISSION

Our mission is to educate and inspire people to appreciate the rarest creatures on Earth. Through accurate information and stunning visuals, Wildpedia aims to raise awareness about endangered and lesser-known animals, encouraging everyone to take part in protecting wildlife and preserving biodiversity.

VISION

Our vision is a world where every animal no matter how rare or unknown is valued, respected, and protected. We envision a future where knowledge leads to action, and humanity lives in harmony with the natural world.
